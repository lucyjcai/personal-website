Lucy Cai

Hi! I'm a current MEng student at MIT. I'm pursuing a concentration in AI and conducting research in the Learning and Intelligent Systems group at CSAIL advised by Leslie Pack Kaelbling. My current project focuses on the problem of interactive object search and how to get robots to most efficiently explore and interact with an environment to locate an object that might be hidden from plain sight. I graduated from computer science (course 6-3) at MIT this past Spring.

In the past, I've also worked on FPGA development for brain-computer interface pipelines. In general, I'm interested in anything that sounds futuristic.
